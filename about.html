About The Modern Frame

Founded in 2010, The Modern Frame was established with a vision to bring contemporary art to the forefront of the cultural conversation. We believe in the power of art to inspire, challenge, and transform. Our gallery is dedicated to showcasing both emerging talents and established artists from around the globe.

"Art is not what you see, but what you make others see." - Edgar Degas
